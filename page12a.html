From the Siddha Orgyenpa:
Isn't the actual practice of mahamudra to sieze hold of the adventious defilements? The person who has eliminated the so-called adventitious defilements has completed the path. Do not deviate from this advice. The key point of the Six Yogas of Tilopa is to apply this.
