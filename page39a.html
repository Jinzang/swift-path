From the Glorious Jigme Dragpa (Abhayakirti): All phenomena are but appearances. All thinking is nothing but mind itself. Appearances are the clarity and what is experienced is mind itself. Also he said: Intrinsic awareness is just freedom from elaboration. Appearances are empty and clarity is empty. They are inseparable as emptiness, like a reflection in water.
